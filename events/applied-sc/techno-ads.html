    Techno-ads

    JECRC TechnoAds is a technical event in which every team will be assigned a product to design a logo. Teams whose logo will be selected after scrutiny are allowed to participate in next round. In second round , the team has to make a punch line and prepare a jingle for that product. Team has to perform jingle within the time limit of 3 minutes


  • 1. Team should be of 2-3 members.
  • 2. Use of internet connection is prohibited.
  • 3. In first round 45 minutes will be given for logo designing and the top 12 teams will be qualified for 2nd round.
  • 4. After scrutiny selected teams will go for second round of jingle performance.
  • 5. Only 3 minutes will be alloted per team for jingle performance.
  • 6. The team with best logo and jingle performance will be awarded.
