    Pic-A-Speako

    This event is based on the fact that “Every Picture tells a tale”. It is a picture description contest which tests the power of observation and the ability to describe it in an effective manner. It shows the potential indulging in an individual for imagination, creativity, presence of mind with visual scenes. Observation of visual clippings creates the new story which should be fictional but should exhibit the substantial correlation. The ability to enact the given situation is checked in the second round. Also, the ability of spontaneous guesses would be judged in the finale round.


  • Round 1: The first participant would be shown pictures for 20 seconds and he/she will have to speak on it for 60 seconds, showing his/her imagination. Then, another picture would be projected for 20 seconds for the other participant and he/she will have to do same as earlier, with some relation to previous picture.

  • Round2: EVER WONDERED...What would happen when SUPERMAN meets JETHALAL? Stop wondering, start creating the scene! Pick any 2 characters from a set given to you, and in a given preparation time of 10 minutes, create a bunch of some action-packed dialogues. STOP STOP STOP, your mimicry or your acting wouldn’t be tested here, your creativity and mind skills would be. The time limit will be two minutes and it is not subject to any extension.

  • Round 3: A set of pictures will be displayed on the projector, for a single team at a time, which will be forming a word. All teams will be given a single set of pictures to decode and the winner will be decided on the basis of least time taken to decode. The team will have to ring the buzzer as soon as the picture is decoded and the word should be written in the sheet provided.
