    Tech Probe

    Tech Probe is a technical quiz based event held in JECRC Renaissance on 17th of march,2019 previous year between 9:30 to 12:30 at C501, C BLOCK. Renaissance is annual techno cultural fest of JECRC, Jaipur. Tech Probe is a quiz event in which 33 teams participated. These teams were from different branches. This quiz was completed in four rounds. Out of these four rounds two were of technical quiz and two were of Fun rounds. The first round was fun round in which one participant was blindfolded and need to draw the logo of the whiteboard as per the instructions given by other team member. Second round was of buzzer based quiz. Third round was of dumbcharades. After third round we have wildcard entry round in which eliminated teams took part for final round. Fourth round was of online technical quiz event Competition seeks to develop technical knowledge among the students which will be helpful for their placements as well as for different competitions like GATE.


  • 1. Only team entries are eligible.
  • 2. Team shall contain 2 members only.
  • 3. The decision of the quiz-master will be final and will not be subjected to any change.
  • 4. One participant will use a smartphone buzzer app for answering questions.
  • 5. Other participants shall not be allowed to use mobile or other electronic instruments.
  • 6. The questions will be in the form of MCQ’s or specific answer.
  • 7. Audience shall not give any clue to the competitors.
  • 8. Teams can’t be modified in any case.
  • 9. A question will be fired & then the team who presses the buzzer first will get to answer it within 5 seconds.
  • 10. In case of tie between 2 or more teams, further 5 questions would be asked for final selection.
