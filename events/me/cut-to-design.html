    Cut to Design

    Cut2design has the main motto to go through the practical aspects Machine Drawing. In this regard, we have enclosed this event with 2 rounds. First round is for checking the theoretical knowledge about Machine drawing basics, after first round about shortlisted candidates will make 3-D object of a given 2-D view.


  • Team of 2 members participation.

  • ROUND 1:
  • Theoretical quiz consisting 20 questions in 20 minutes.
  • Those clearing the first round will go to next round.

  • ROUND 2:
  • 2D view will be consisting of elevation, plan and side view with the help of this drawing students will have to carve 3D Object in Soap.
